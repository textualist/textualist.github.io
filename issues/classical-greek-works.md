## Notable Gaps

Works by these authors that are **not** on Project Gutenberg in English:

**Aristotle:**
- Metaphysics
- Rhetoric
- On the Soul (De Anima)
- Prior Analytics / Posterior Analytics
- Physics
- On the Heavens (De Caelo)
- On Generation and Corruption
- Eudemian Ethics
- Topics
- On Sophistical Refutations

**Homer:**
- Homeric Hymns (no standalone English edition found)
- Batrachomyomachia (Battle of the Frogs and Mice)

**Plato:**
- Hippias Major (available only in Jowett's collected Dialogues Vol. 2, #76464)
- Clitophon
- Epinomis
- Letters
